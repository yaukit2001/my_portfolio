Final Year Project

I am honored to have received both the Bronze Award at the Malaysia Technology Expo (MTE) 2023 and the prestigious 2nd runner-up position in the IEEE Malaysia Section Competition 2022, all thanks to the exceptional quality of my final year project and the invaluable guidance of my lecturer.

*** Smart Walking Frame is the title use to apply IEEE Competition where Stride Assist is the title use to apply MTE Competition

Smart Walking Frame (StrideAssists) is an Internet of Things project which mainly integrate Arduino Uno and mobile application to the walking frame. Beside aid and provide stability and safety when people have to move from one point to another point, user can click on the button which embedded on the walking frame to activate different functions. For instance, alarm function, SOS function, obstacle and water detection function. Apart from this, the mobile application can perform live location tracking and set as many as possible contact number as emergency contact. With these features provided, it can help those who unable moving easily or lack of self-defence capability people such as amputees and elderly.
